About the Case

Human or A.I.? is a short detective-style quiz built for our hackathon. You'll be shown text, images, and video clips — some made by people, some generated by AI — and asked to spot which is which.

Every round teaches you a real clue: odd hands in images, too-perfect grammar in text, mismatched lip-sync in video. The goal isn't just a score — it's a sharper eye for AI-made media.
